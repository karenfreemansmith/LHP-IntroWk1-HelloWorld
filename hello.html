Hello World

A page to greet the planet

This page is an attempt to greet everyone on earth using various human languages.

We'll start with the languages that are most familiar.

  • English: Hello, World!
  • Spanish: Hola, mundo!
  • French: Bonjour, monde!
  • Japanese: Konichiwa, sekai!
  • German: Guten tag, Welt!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
  • Latin: Salve, mundum!
  • Pig Latin: ellow-hay, orld-way!
